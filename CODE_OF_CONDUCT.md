# Code of Conduct

CmdZ is committed to providing a welcoming, safe, and respectful environment for everyone who participates in the project.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, constructive, and patient.
- Discuss ideas and code without attacking the people behind them.
- Respect differing experiences, viewpoints, and identities.
- Accept feedback gracefully and take responsibility for mistakes.
- Put the health of the project and its community first.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, personal attacks, sexualized conduct, deliberate disruption, and publishing another person's private information without permission are not acceptable.

## Scope

This policy applies in the repository, issue tracker, pull requests, and other spaces where someone is representing the CmdZ project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer using the contact information on their [GitHub profile](https://github.com/patrykchojecki). If no private contact method is available, open a minimal issue requesting a private reporting channel without including sensitive details.

Reports will be reviewed fairly and kept confidential to the extent reasonably possible. Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this policy.

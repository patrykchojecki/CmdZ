# CmdZ Support

## Shortcut not working

Chrome can leave a suggested shortcut unassigned when it conflicts with a browser or extension command.

1. Open `chrome://extensions/shortcuts`.
2. Find **CmdZ**.
3. Assign **Command + Z** to **Reopen the most recently closed tab**.

Chrome or editable page elements may still take priority because **Command + Z** is the standard Undo shortcut.

## Report a problem

[Open a GitHub issue](https://github.com/patrykchojecki/CmdZ/issues/new) and include:

- your macOS version;
- your Chrome version;
- whether the shortcut appears in `chrome://extensions/shortcuts`; and
- the steps needed to reproduce the problem.

CmdZ supports Google Chrome 96 or newer on macOS.
